Insurance & Fees

Here’s what to expect for payment, insurance, and telehealth logistics. We’ll confirm benefits and costs together before starting so there are no surprises.

Insurance

I am in-network with:

  • Aetna
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • MedCost

Coverage and copays vary by plan. We’ll confirm benefits before starting so there are no surprises.

Private Pay & Sliding Scale

For clients without insurance or who prefer not to use it, I offer self-pay options and an income-based sliding scale.

We can talk through what’s sustainable for you during the consultation call.

Logistics

  • Telehealth-first sessions for residents of North Carolina, with limited in-person home visits after admission
  • Children 5+, teens, adults, couples, and families
  • Payment options include major credit cards and HSA/FSA cards
Rates & frequency

Transparent pricing so you can plan ahead.

Most clients begin with weekly sessions and taper to biweekly or monthly as progress holds.

A Good Faith Estimate is available to every client as required by the No Surprises Act. We’ll review it during the intake process.

If I’m out-of-network

Superbills & reimbursement options.

Many clients submit receipts to their insurance for partial reimbursement. I’m happy to provide monthly superbills and collaborate with HSAs or FSAs.

Before we begin

Call the number on the back of your insurance card and ask about telehealth, out-of-network benefits, deductibles, and the reimbursement rate for CPT code 90837.

During therapy

Pay with a card on file; receipts or superbills arrive in your secure portal. You submit them directly to your insurance if desired.

Need help?

I can connect you with reimbursement resources such as Reimbursify or provide scripts for speaking with your insurer.
