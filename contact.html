After you reach out

Here’s what happens next.

I reply within two business days. During the consult we’ll talk about your hopes for therapy, scheduling needs, and whether we’re a good fit. If another resource seems better, I’ll offer referrals.

Emergency resources

If you’re in crisis, please contact one of the following:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text)
  • 911 or go to the nearest emergency room
  • Call the Mobile Crisis Hotline at 1-866-275-3662

Secure communication

All forms on this site use encrypted delivery through EmailJS. Once you become a client, we’ll communicate through a HIPAA-compliant portal for scheduling, forms, and telehealth links.

Accessibility

Captions are available for video sessions, and I’m happy to adjust session length, lighting, or pacing to support sensory needs. Please share what would help you feel comfortable.
