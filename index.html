Why Mountains to Sea

Therapy that feels like coming home to yourself.

Clients often say our work together feels grounded, practical, and genuinely compassionate. Whether I'm helping a child understand their big feelings or supporting an adult through trauma, I blend real clinical skill with authentic, human care.

Body-aware & relational

I listen to what your nervous system is saying—the tension in your shoulders, the pace of your breath, what lives in your body. We move at a pace that helps your system feel safe.

Family-systems informed

I understand how family patterns, attachment stories, and relationship dynamics shape who we become. We look at the bigger picture—your relationships, your support system, the patterns you've inherited.

Flexible & collaborative

I invite your feedback every session. Some weeks we process and go deep; other weeks we build practical skills or talk through what's coming up. We shape the work together.

Support across seasons

Therapy can help when...

For kids & teens

  • Emotions feel “too big” for their body
  • They’re masking at school and melting at home
  • Transitions like adoption, divorce, or a move need space
  • They’ve been through scary or confusing experiences

For adults

  • Anxiety or burnout keeps hijacking your days
  • You’re carrying complex trauma, grief, or religious harm
  • Your relationships feel tense, distant, or stuck
  • You want therapy that values both intuition and evidence

For parents & caregivers

  • You’re craving regulation strategies you can actually use
  • Behavior plans haven’t accounted for nervous systems
  • Co-parenting, foster care, or kinship care is overwhelming
  • You need a therapist who respects your expertise on your kid
What to expect

Getting started is simple.

  1. Reach out

    Send a message or request a consult and share what is bringing you to therapy right now.

  2. Free consult

    We spend 15 minutes talking through logistics, goals, and any immediate questions.

  3. First sessions

    We build safety, explore your history, and map the strengths already present.

  4. Ongoing care

    We adapt the pace, modalities, and focus as you grow, integrating regular check-ins.
